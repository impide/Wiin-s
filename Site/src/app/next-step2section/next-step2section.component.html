Why Wiin's ?

The increase of applications, the emergence of advertising and the lack of privacy have led us to develop Wiin's : the solution that meets the current usages.

If you can imagine an application where you can share your photo content with other users while listening to music or watching videos while chatting with your friends.

All this, without advertising and with the possibility to use cryptocurrency to help artists you like.

This project emerged in 2017 in in the minds of three people who shared the same values and the same vision.
