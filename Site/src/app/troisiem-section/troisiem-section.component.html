Verification / Certification

Any content creator who wishes to be paid for their audio and audiovisual content and have monetary gifts must apply for certification.

To be certified, you must first be accepted at the verification, then transmit his address Crypto-currency.

Monetary Gifts

Each certified content creator can receive monetary gifts in cryptocurrencies. These gifts can be sent by other users of the application.

Users reward artists and content creators by using their money stored on the platform and simply selecting an artist and an amount
